Spooky Marshmallows

Description

Here's how to make spooky ghost themed marshmallows.

Ingredients

  • Marshmallows
  • Black food coloring

Steps

  1. Pick out your favorite marshmallows from the bag.
  2. Make eyes on the marshmallows using the food coloring.
  3. Make a mouth on the marshmallows using the food coloring.
  4. They are now marshmallow ghosts, enjoy!
