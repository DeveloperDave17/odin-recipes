Monster Mac and Cheese

Description

Here's how to make the spookiest mac and cheese alive. You should do the monster mash while making the Monster Mac and Cheese.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Green cheese
  • Eyeball candy
  • Monster milk
  • Monster flour
  • Monster butter

Steps

  1. Pre heat the oven very high.
  2. Add macaroni to boiling water and let boil for 8 minutes.
  3. Melt the monster butter and mix with the monster flour.
  4. whisk monster milk into the butter and flour mixture. Then add melted green cheese, stir aggressively.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until it looks spooky and the macaroni and cheese is screaming, doesn't take too long.
